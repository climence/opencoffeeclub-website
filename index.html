The Open Coffee Club was created in London to help entrepreneurs, developers and investors to meet in an informal environment in the real world to discuss and exchange views about internet and IT related issues. Success factors : a place, a time, and a day. The OpenCoffee Wellington are taking place every Tuesday at 9am @ In Good Company Space.

- Who should come?
Eveyone.
The idea is not to meet someone specific but to offer a meeting opportunity to strangers to meet outside of the office or of a private club

- Why ?
Everyday, we hear : investors are unreachable, entrepreneurs need a place to meet, it's a pity we have to attend trade shows to meet potential parteners. The Open Coffee fills those needs offering an informal space for everyone to meet.

- What can you do?
If you are an investor or an entrepreneur, come, chat, share and talk about the Open Coffee Club Wellington around you.



* Created by Saul Klein in London in March 2007, the Open Coffee Club is happening in more than a 100 cities around the world, come and meet us and HAVE FUN !
